Campaign and solus content logic used to collect birthday data from customers who have not yet provided their date of birth. This is a content insertion mechanism rather than a triggered automation sequence.

Birthday capture within campaigns and solus sends

Existing and new customers who have not provided a birthday are identified via profile filtering, then shown a birthday-capture content block inside email sends. The objective is to gather date-of-birth data and unlock the birthday reward journey later.

Content handoff

Core message

“Tell us your Birthday” and “Don’t miss out on your Birthday Treat” act as the primary capture copy.

Use case

This asset should be used within BAU campaigns or dedicated solus placements where birthday enrichment is the objective.
